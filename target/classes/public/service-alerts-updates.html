News & Updates
Service alert: Potential delays throughout Atlantic region following Hurricane Fiona
We are executing contingency plans to maintain service following Hurricane Fiona. The storm is expected to hit the Atlantic region this weekend.
Peak Surcharges
One or more Peak Surcharge(s) will apply to any shipment or piece travelling through TX Courier’s network using a corresponding Applicable Service during the identified Peak Period, in accordance with the details described below.
Service alert: Weather events potentially impacting service
Throughout United States, ongoing extreme seasonal weather events can potentially cause disruptions to our service, resulting in uncontrollable service delays. 
Resuming written signature process for “Signature Required” shipments
As of Aug. 20, 2022, we are resuming our pre-pandemic process to capture a written signature for the delivery of all Signature Required shipments. Customers will be asked to provide a written signature on the courier’s scanner at time the package is being delivered.

Please be advised TX Courier is making certain modifications to our TX Courier Terms and Conditions

Service alert: Belarus, Ukraine and Russia
Due to the current situation throughout Ukraine and Russia, effective immediately, our service to the regions has been suspended until further notice.
TX Courier 2022 rate adjustment for LTL (Freight) services
Over the past year, TX Courier has helped our customers adapt to the unprecedented impacts COVID-19 has had on consumers and businesses of all sizes across the globe.
Consumer Alert: Fraudulent representation of TX Courier careers website
TX Courier is aware of several individuals who have received job offers after having applied for jobs posted to a fraudulent website that appears to mimic the TX Courier careers page. The job postings and subsequent offer letters are not from TX Courier. We believe this is likely part of a fraudulent phishing campaign. We are investigating the situation and have worked with authorities to take down the offending website. We have also contacted the Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre.
