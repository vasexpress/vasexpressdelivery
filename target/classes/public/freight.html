Freight shipments require a TX Courier shipping label or TX Courier-approved bar code shipping label as well as a bill of lading. For information on how to properly label your freight shipment, see our Pallets page.

For size and weight restrictions refer to our Packaging Guidelines.

Once you have set up your freight account, please contact our Technical Support team at +1 (716) 327-5471 to set up a login ID and password for your online account so that you can ship online.
For a free quote on your freight, contact us. info@giaglobalcourier.com

Both shipping and freight are the transportation of goods either by air, land, or sea domestically or internationally. Freight and shipping are both used for the bulk transportation of goods, however, freight always refers to a larger quantity of goods. Freight is used for shipments exceeding 150 lbs. Freight is most commonly used for commercial purposes and can be more cost-effective when shipping large quantities.
